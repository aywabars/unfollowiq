Why does this take so long?

Instagram actively monitors for automated behavior. Accounts that unfollow too quickly get temporarily banned or permanently restricted.

UnfollowIQ protects your account by:

Human-like timing — Each unfollow waits a random amount of time (sometimes 25s, sometimes 90s) to mimic how a real person would browse. No two delays are the same.

Daily limits — Your account tier determines how many unfollows per day are safe. After hitting the limit, we pause for several hours before the next batch.

Smart pacing — If Instagram sends a rate-limit signal, we automatically back off and wait before retrying.

Think of it like unfollowing manually — it just takes time, but your account stays safe.
